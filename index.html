How to Use This App
Complete setup guide for Ma Pao and students
Setup guide
For Ma Pao — logging in & access
1
No separate login needed. This app is a standalone HTML file — open it in any browser on any device (laptop, tablet, phone). No account, no password, no install.
Save this file to your desktop or Google Drive. Double-click to open in your browser before each class.
2
The AI brain is powered by Claude (Anthropic). When you click "Generate Question" or "Generate AI email", the app contacts the Claude API. This requires an internet connection.
3
Students do not log in. You open the app, select the student's name in the sidebar, and run the session. No student ever needs to touch a password.
For Ma Pao — running an art session
1
Click a student's name in the sidebar. Their chapter list loads instantly.
2
In the Chapters tab, click the chapter you are working on today. Then switch to AI Session.
3
Click ✦ Generate Question. The AI reads the student's grade level and the selected chapter, then creates an original question sourced from Augsburg's book. It never gives the answer.
4
Read the question aloud. The student draws on paper. Evaluate their drawing, then click Mark correct, Partially correct, or Needs reteaching.
5
If stuck, switch Reteach Mode — Visual → Verbal → Kinetic — and try again. Use Give hint sparingly. The app tracks consecutive hints and flags overuse.
6
For parent updates: go to Parent Reports and click ✦ Generate AI email. A full, friendly, ready-to-send email will appear instantly.
For students — what they experience
1
Students hear the question read aloud by Ma Pao. They draw on paper — the screen is just for the teacher.
2
For A.L. (Gr. 9–12): she can read the question herself and use the reteach options independently — great for building self-directed learning.
3
For L.J. (Kinder · logic Gr. 3): questions use Kindergarten-friendly language, but the reteach strategies go deeper to match his strong reasoning. The AI adapts both ways.
